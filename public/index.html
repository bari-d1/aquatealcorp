The Program

What This Bootcamp Is About

Most training programs teach theory. This bootcamp focuses on real-world, practical skills used on actual Canadian construction projects.

You will work on real scenarios, use industry templates, and practice reviewing RFIs and change orders - exactly the way it's done on the job.

Key Topics Covered

✔ Construction contracts and risk allocation
✔ RFI management and submittal tracking
✔ Change orders, payment certification, and holdbacks
✔ Claims, disputes, and project closeout

Curriculum

What You Will Learn

Contracts & Risk

Understand construction contracts and how risk is allocated between parties.

Drawings & Specs

Interpret drawings and specifications used on Canadian projects.

RFIs & Submittals

Manage RFIs and track submittals and shop drawings effectively.

Change Orders

Evaluate change orders, variations, and their cost implications.

Payment Certification

Understand payment certification processes and statutory holdbacks.

Claims & Disputes

Identify, manage, and document claims and construction disputes.

Project Closeout

Handle all closeout documentation correctly and efficiently.

Industry Templates

Use actual templates from the field, not just theory from a textbook.
